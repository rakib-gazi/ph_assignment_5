Donate for Flood at Noakhali, Bangladesh

Noakhali is facing a severe flood crisis following days of heavy rain and rising river waters. The flooding has impacted thousands, with homes submerged and crops devastated across large areas. Many roads are now impassable, cutting off communities and leading to shortages of essential goods. Local authorities and relief organizations are working together to provide food, clean water, and temporary shelter for those affected. Despite these efforts, the situation remains dire as floodwaters have yet to recede, and more aid is urgently needed to support the displaced and prevent further damage.
